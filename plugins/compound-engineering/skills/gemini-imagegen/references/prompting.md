Prompting best practices for different image types with Gemini.
</overview>

<style name="photorealistic">
## Photorealistic Scenes

Include camera details: lens type, lighting, angle, mood.

**Example:**
> "A photorealistic close-up portrait, 85mm lens, soft golden hour light, shallow depth of field"

**Key elements:**
- Lens (35mm, 50mm, 85mm, etc.)
- Lighting (golden hour, soft box, natural, dramatic)
- Depth of field (shallow, deep)
- Camera angle (close-up, wide shot, aerial)
</style>

<style name="stylized">
## Stylized Art

Specify style explicitly.

**Example:**
> "A kawaii-style sticker of a happy red panda, bold outlines, cel-shading, white background"

**Key elements:**
- Art style (kawaii, minimalist, retro, geometric)
- Line work (bold outlines, soft edges, no outlines)
- Shading (cel-shading, gradient, flat)
- Background (white, transparent look, solid color)
</style>

<style name="text">
## Text in Images

Be explicit about font style and placement.

**Example:**
> "Create a logo with text 'Daily Grind' in clean sans-serif, black and white, coffee bean motif"

**Key elements:**
- Exact text (in quotes)
- Font style (sans-serif, serif, handwritten, bold)
- Color scheme
- Positioning/placement
- Supporting visual elements
</style>

<style name="product">
## Product Mockups

Describe lighting setup and surface.

**Example:**
> "Studio-lit product photo on polished concrete, three-point softbox setup, 45-degree angle"

**Key elements:**
- Surface (marble, wood, concrete, white backdrop)
- Lighting (studio, natural, three-point)
- Angle (overhead, 45-degree, eye-level)
- Shadows (soft, hard, minimal)
</style>

<style name="logos">
## Logos and Branding

Focus on simplicity and clarity.

**Example:**
> "Minimalist logo for tech startup 'NexaFlow', flowing geometric shapes suggesting data movement, navy blue and silver, works at all sizes"

**Key elements:**
- Company name (exact spelling)
- Style (minimalist, vintage, modern, playful)
- Colors
- Symbolic elements
- Scalability consideration
</style>

<style name="stickers">
## Stickers and Icons

Emphasize clear shapes and outlines.

**Example:**
> "Die-cut sticker design of a smiling coffee cup with steam forming a heart, thick white border, bright colors, kawaii style"

**Key elements:**
- Subject with clear outline
- Border style (thick white, thin, none)
- Color palette
- Die-cut shape indication
- Style (kawaii, flat, 3D)
</style>
